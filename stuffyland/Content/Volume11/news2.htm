Interview With Dr. GretaI

Here is the interview we promised on the cover page.

Dr.Greta: Oh hi!

Stuffyland News Reporter: I have some questions to ask. Will you answer them?

Dr. Greta: Of coarse!

SN reporter: How did you feel when you found your first Throaty Frog?

Dr.Greta: Amazing! It was like everything in the world stopped at that moment. For a few seconds I couldn't move.

SN reporter: What was the first sound your Throaty Frog was able to copy?

Dr. Greta:A cat's meow.My cat Fluffy meowed when I first brought my Throaty Frog home in a tank.

SN reporter: How did you feel when the National Association of Science gave you permission to go looking for more Throaty Frogs?j

Dr. Greta: I was elated. And my team that helps me is always so helpful.

SN reporter: Well, thanks.

Dr. Greta: Good-by.
