Interview with Famous Actress

Crystal Shannon, the actress who is Shaline in the movie Pencils for Everyone, had an interview with a Stuffyland reporter. Listen in!

Stuffyland News reporter: Hi Crystal! I was expecting you to be older. How old are you?

Crystal: 8, but I’m about to turn 9 in 3 weeks.

SN reporter: Why did they choose someone so young?

Crystal: You see the director wanted someone young, but with a voice that was naturally scratchy. I fit that description perfectly.

SN reporter: How does being in a star role feel like?

Crystal: Well, obviously it is nerve-racking, but it was fun as well. The director makes a point to make sure I am happy and well-fed.

SN reporter: How about the set?

Crystal: Oh, the set. It is so amazing! And the best part is my costume. I love it so much!

SN reporter: Do you like your co-stars, Mike Malloy and Kit Thompson?

Crystal: They are my best friends! We get along very well, considering that they have to share a room with me.

SN reporter: What do you do between practices and filming?

Crystal: Read books, watch other shows, hang out with my friends. In other words, what the average 8-year old stuffy does- but I use a limo.

SN reporter: How about school?

Crystal: 5 days a month I go to regular school with my friends, but otherwise I’m with a tutor on the set.
